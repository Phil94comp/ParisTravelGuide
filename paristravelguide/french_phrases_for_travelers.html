French Phrases for Travelers

Helpful French Phrases



Hello...Bonjour...(Bohn-joohr)

Yes...Oui...(wee)

No...Non...(Nohn)

I'm sorry. I don't speak French. Do you speak English?...Je suis désolé. Je ne parle pas français. Parlez vous anglais?... (Zhuh swee deh-zo-ley. Zhe nuh pahrl pa frahn-say. Pahr-lay voo ahn-glay?)

I would like...Je voudrais...(Juh voo-dray...)



Reasons to Learn French Phrases



  • It is respectful to speak to someone in France in French and ask if they know English.
  • Not everyone in Paris speaks English, particularly outside the tourist areas.


Helpful Tips for Paris Travelers



  • If French people can speak English, they will do so rather than expecting a tourist to speak French.
  • There are online tools to learn and practice French.

DuoLingo is a free website to learn French.

Babbel requires a subscription to learn French. The price decreases depending on the length of the subscription, with longer subscriptions costing less per month. There is also an option to purchase a lifetime subscription.
