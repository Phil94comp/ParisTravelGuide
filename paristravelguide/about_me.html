About Me

Background About the Author



My name is Phillip Andrews. I have traveled extensively throughout the United States, Canada, and Europe.

I have traveled to France, Spain, England, Scotland, Wales, Germany, Italy, Switzerland, Austria, Belgium and the Netherlands.

My family and I enjoy planning vacations together. We create our own itinerary and do not use tour groups. I have traveled to Europe four times and to Paris, France twice.

I enjoy local cuisine from the countries I visit.

I have had a few unique experiences I wasn't expecting, such as going to the Ommegang Festival, in Brussels, Belgium. The current version of the festival is based on the 1549 celebration where Emperor Charles V visited the area to introduce his son, Phillip II. The festival includes jousting and a large parade through the city.

I am planning to travel to Asia in the future.
